Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about booking and traveling

Booking with TunisiaBus takes just 3 simple steps:

  1. Enter your departure city, destination, and travel date
  2. Select from available buses with seat maps
  3. Complete secure payment to receive instant e-ticket

Our platform supports 200+ operators across Tunisia with real-time availability.

Our flexible booking options allow:

  • Free cancellations up to 24 hours before departure (for most operators)
  • Easy rescheduling through your account dashboard
  • Instant refunds processed within 3-5 business days

Specific policies vary by operator - check your booking confirmation for details.

We accept all major payment options with bank-level security:

  • Visa/Mastercard credit/debit cards
  • PayPal and digital wallets
  • Bank transfers
  • Mobile payment systems

All transactions are PCI-DSS compliant with 256-bit encryption.

For most operators, mobile tickets are sufficient:

  • Show your QR code e-ticket on your smartphone
  • Ensure your device is charged for boarding
  • Have valid ID matching your booking

About 15% of operators still require printed tickets - check your booking confirmation for requirements.
